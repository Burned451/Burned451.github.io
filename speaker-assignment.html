Problems and Solutions

Problems Solutions
Printing takes too long Split the speaker in half and print both halves separately
Wire terminal didn't print Drill hole through speaker base above first hole
Magnet holder clips broke off Glue magnet to speaker base
Voice coil not staying in place Borrow super glue and glue the top and bottom
Terminal screws won't fit in holes Screw in large screws from exterior to make the holes even bigger

Construction Process

  1. Modeling the parts
  2. Printing the parts
  3. Attatching the cone to the top
  4. Attatching the magnets to the base
  5. Wrapping the voice coil
  6. Screwing in the wire terminals
  7. Glueing the top and bottom together
